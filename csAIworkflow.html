AI Procurement Workflow System

Transforming manual procurement processes for a Singapore-based FMCG client through AI-powered automation, reducing cycle time by 60% and improving data accuracy to 95%.

Client Singapore FMCG
Duration 6 Months
Team Size 8 Members
Role Project Manager
Industry FMCG

Executive Summary

The client, a leading FMCG company in Singapore, faced significant challenges with their manual procurement process. Purchase orders were processed through emails and spreadsheets, leading to delays, errors, and lack of visibility. We designed and delivered an AI-powered procurement workflow system that automated the entire process from requisition to payment, resulting in 60% time savings, 40% cost reduction, and 95% data accuracy.

Client Background

A mid-sized FMCG company with operations across Southeast Asia, processing 500+ purchase orders monthly across 15 categories. The company was growing rapidly but their procurement processes had not scaled accordingly.

Business Problem

  • Manual procurement process taking 5–7 days per purchase order
  • High error rate in purchase order data entry (15–20%)
  • No real-time visibility into procurement status
  • Lack of vendor performance tracking
  • Compliance and audit challenges

As-Is vs To-Be Process

As-Is Process

Manual email-based workflow with multiple handoffs


To-Be Process

Automated AI-driven workflow with real-time tracking
