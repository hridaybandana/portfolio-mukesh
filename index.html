Featured Case Study

AI Procurement Workflow System

Transforming manual procurement processes for a Singapore-based FMCG client through AI-powered automation, reducing cycle time by 60% and improving data accuracy to 95%.

ClientSingapore FMCG
Duration6 Months
Team Size8 Members
RoleProject Manager
IndustryFMCG

Executive Summary

The client, a leading FMCG company in Singapore, faced significant challenges with their manual procurement process. Purchase orders were processed through emails and spreadsheets, leading to delays, errors, and lack of visibility. We designed and delivered an AI-powered procurement workflow system that automated the entire process from requisition to payment, resulting in 60% time savings, 40% cost reduction, and 95% data accuracy.

Client Background

A mid-sized FMCG company with operations across Southeast Asia, processing 500+ purchase orders monthly across 15 categories. The company was growing rapidly but their procurement processes had not scaled accordingly.

Business Problem

  • Manual procurement process taking 5-7 days per purchase order
  • High error rate in purchase order data entry (15-20%)
  • No real-time visibility into procurement status
  • Lack of vendor performance tracking
  • Compliance and audit challenges

As-Is vs To-Be Process

As-Is Process Diagram Placeholder

Manual email-based workflow with multiple handoffs

To-Be Process Diagram Placeholder

Automated AI-driven workflow with real-time tracking

Discovery & Requirements

Stakeholders

Procurement Head, Finance Team, Department Heads, Vendors, IT Team, Executive Sponsors

Business Analysis

Workshops, interviews, process mapping, gap analysis, and requirements prioritization using MoSCoW

Functional Requirements

PO creation, approval workflows, vendor management, invoice processing, reporting dashboards

Non-Functional Requirements

99.9% uptime, sub-2s response time, SOC 2 compliance, multi-region support

Architecture & Technology

System Architecture Diagram Placeholder

Microservices architecture with Azure cloud services

Comprehensive training for 100+ end users

Lessons Learned

  • Early stakeholder alignment is critical for AI adoption success
  • Phased rollout reduces risk and builds user confidence
  • Comprehensive training drives adoption and reduces support tickets
  • Continuous feedback loops enable iterative improvement

Future Roadmap

  • Expand AI capabilities to vendor performance prediction
  • Integrate with ERP systems for end-to-end automation
  • Add mobile-first experience for field procurement teams
  • Implement advanced analytics for strategic sourcing
